About Me

Hi. I'm Fernando Jurado-Lasso.

I have recently completed my PhD in Engineering within the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) at The University of Melbourne, Australia, under the supervision of Prof. Ampalavanapillai Nirmalathas. During my PhD, I investigated the challenges and benefits of Software-Defined Wireless Sensor Networks (SDWSNs) as a solution to intelligent and flexible resource management and run-time reconfiguration of WSNs.

Research

My research interests are in the broaden area of Internet of Things (IoT) including software-defined WSNs, machine learning in the context of IoT, embedded operating systems, and validations by means of testbeds.

I am passionate about devising solutions to enable intelligent and flexible resource management of WSNs, making them reprogrammable.

Below are the contributions that I have made in the above-mentioned research topic.
